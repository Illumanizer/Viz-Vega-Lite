Part A — Required Charts

1. Donut — Revenue by Rep

Hover a slice to see revenue and share of total

2. Streamgraph — Revenue over Time by Item

Click an item in the legend to highlight its stream

3. Trellis Histogram — Units by Item

Dashed red line shows mean units per item; hover bars for counts

Part B — Interactive Charts

4. Order Timeline

Drag to brush a time window — bar chart below updates. Slider filters min order value. Red dashed line = trend.

5. Revenue Heatmap — Region × Item

Click a cell to select that item; monthly trend line updates below. Double-click to reset.

Part D — Additional Visualizations

6. Cumulative Revenue by Item

Running total per item over time. Use the Year dropdown to zoom into a single year.

7. Scatter Matrix — Units, Unit Cost, Revenue

All pairwise scatter plots. Drag to brush any panel and matching points highlight across all panels.

8. Boxplot — Units Distribution by Item

Shows median, IQR and outliers. Use the Region dropdown to filter.

Part C — Multiview (all 8 charts concatenated)

Cross-filter by Region: click any Region bar on the left to filter all charts simultaneously. Double-click to reset.

All 8 Charts — Single Vega-Lite Spec

vconcat / hconcat / facet — region selection shared across all views
